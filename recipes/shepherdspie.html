Shepherd's Pie

Shepherd's Pie is a classic comfort food that hails from the British Isles, traditionally made with minced lamb and topped with creamy mashed potatoes. This dish is a hearty and satisfying meal, perfect for chilly evenings. The rich, savory filling is often enhanced with vegetables like carrots and peas, creating a balanced and flavorful experience. The golden, crispy potato topping adds a delightful texture, making each bite a comforting reminder of home-cooked meals. Whether enjoyed on a family gathering or as a cozy dinner for one, Shepherd's Pie remains a beloved staple in many households.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 50g butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the minced lamb over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and garlic to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are softened.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, salt, and pepper. Add the frozen peas and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, boil the potatoes in salted water until tender. Drain and mash with butter and milk until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Spread the lamb mixture in a baking dish and top with the mashed potatoes. Use a fork to create a textured surface.
  7. If desired, sprinkle grated cheese on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  9. Let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your Shepherd's Pie!
