Pasta Pomodoro al Audrey Hepburn

Inspired by the timeless elegance and refined simplicity of Audrey Hepburn, this Pasta Pomodoro is a celebration of fresh, high-quality ingredients. It's not about complex layers of flavor, but rather the harmonious interplay of sun-ripened tomatoes, fragrant basil, and the finest olive oil, all brought together with perfectly cooked pasta. This dish embodies the idea that true beauty lies in understated perfection, offering a light yet satisfying meal that is both comforting and sophisticated. It's a dish that feels equally at home at a casual lunch or an elegant dinner, much like Audrey's own versatile charm.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 50ml extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the skillet and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes break down and form a sauce.
  4. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Add fresh basil leaves and stir to combine.
  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the sauce until well coated. Serve immediately, garnished with additional basil and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
